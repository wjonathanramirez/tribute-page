Jaime Escalante

The math teacher that empowered generations

Jaime Escalante and actor Edward James Olmos during the filming of 'Stand And Deliver'


Here's a time line of Escalante's life:


  • 1930 - Born in La Paz, Bolivia.

  • 1973 - Earned his Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics at California State University, Los Angeles

  • 1974 - He began to teach at Garfield High School.

  • 1982 - Escalante gains media attention when 18 of his students passed the AP Calculus exam.

  • 1983 - Number of students enrolling in his Calculus course more than doubled than last year.

  • 1987 - Managed to help 73 students pass the AB version and 12 people pass the BC version at East Los Angeles College.
